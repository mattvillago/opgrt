  1. Please indicate the resection defect using either the “Draw Mode" (preferred) or “Selection Mode” (Drawing Mode: OFF).
  2. The Draw Mode allows you to delineate the resection volume, while simultaneously highlighting involved subunits of the resection defect. The Selection Mode highlights involved subunits without delineating the precise resection edges.
  3. For patients who underwent a mandibulectomy, click on the involved region of the mandible (either in Draw Mode or Selection Mode) to indicate whether the resection was a Marginal Mandibulectomy or Segmental Mandibulectomy.
  4. For patients who underwent a maxillectomy, click on the involved region of the maxilla (either in Draw Mode or Selection Mode) to indicate whether the resection was a Infrastructure or Suprastructure Maxillectomy.
  5. To erase the described defect in Draw Mode or the selected regions in Selection Mode, click on Clear Canvas.
